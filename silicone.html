Professional use of silicone

Silicone service is an essential service in the construction and renovation process, usually used to fill gaps between walls, floors, ceilings, windows, doors, barriers, shutters, and exterior surfaces. Silicone is usually used to fill narrow and small gaps that are difficult to reach with other materials, such as cement or plaster.

its internal features

  • High flexibility
  • Strong adhesion
  • Easy application
  • Fast drying

its external features

  • Leak resistance
  • Resistance to external factors
  • Resistance to chemicals
  • Improve overall appearance

In general, silicone service for filling gaps is a fundamental step in the construction and renovation process, helping to improve the appearance of surfaces and protect them from leakage and seepage, improve the quality of work, and develop the artistic achievements that have been accomplished
